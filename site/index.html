The Challenge

What is transformative AI?

We want AI to make the world better — to accelerate scientific discovery, improve public services, and raise living standards for everyone. But there is real risk that the rapid advance of AI causes economic disruption severe enough to threaten the social contract. The key to ensuring things get better, not worse, is better collective intelligence: tracking what could happen, testing what we can do about it, and holding ourselves accountable for being prepared.
